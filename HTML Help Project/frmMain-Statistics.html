Statistics

When the month is over, it's time for you to try to remember what you've done all month! Not anymore - The "Statistics" tab in Hours and Minutes, spares you from the paper hastle. It can create a report in no-time, just for you to print and give to your boss.

Creating a Report
  1. Bring up the main window. Do this by double-clicking the Hours and Minutes icon in the tray-area of your startmenu. Or do this by rightclicking the tray-area icon, and selecting "Hours and Minutes"

  2. Make sure that the "Statistics" tab is visible.

  3. Select the Task (or all) and timespan that you're interrested in (by default Hours and Minutes select the current month).

  4. Click the "Create" button to create your report. Print it out, or copy it to another program.

  5. When finished viewing the report, click the OK button to return to the main dialog.
