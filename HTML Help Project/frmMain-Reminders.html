Reminders

Reminders are useful for reminding important dates (like your girl/boy-friends birthday), lunch hours, regular meetings, to call someone, etc.

Creating a Reminder

  1. Bring up the main window. Do this by double-clicking the Hours and Minutes icon in the tray-area of your startmenu. Or do this by rightclicking the tray-area icon, and selecting "Hours and Minutes"

  2. Make sure that the "Reminders" tab is visible, rightclick the list and select the "New"-menu.

  3. The wizard that shows will guide you through creating a reminder.
