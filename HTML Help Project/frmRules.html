Rules

Rules are pretty much like reminders, but bound to a specific task. Rules can be setup to remind you after working with a specific task for a specified period of time. There is no limitation of how many rules each task can have.

Scenario

A customer has paid for 3 hours of your valuable time, not more, not less. Create a rule on the task that you've assigned to your customer, make the rule remind you when 3 hours has past in one day.

Creating a Rule
  1. Bring up the main window. Do this by double-clicking the Hours and Minutes icon in the tray-area of your startmenu. Or do this by rightclicking the tray-area icon, and selecting "Hours and Minutes"

  2. Make sure that the "Preferences" tab is visible, then click the "Modify Tasks" button.

  3. Select the task to which you would like to add a rule. (Or click the "New" button to create one). Then click the "Rules" button.

  4. Click the "New" button in the Rules window that just appeared.

  5. Select when you want the rule to trigger, and what you would like to happen.

  6. Move your mouse to the underlined values in the "Rule description" area. Click all undefined values, and enter appropriate values.

  7. Click OK, then OK once again to save the rule to the task.
