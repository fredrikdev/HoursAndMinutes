Hours and Minutes Tour

Do you want to know more exactly where the most of your time is spent? Do your boss need a more detailed report of exactly what it is you're doing? Have you tiered of filling in the timesheet at the end of each month, trying to remember what you've worked with?

Hours and Minutes is designed to do all this for you. The run in the background, logging time - when you're working. Userdefined tasks enables you to log time to different tasks (ie. 'Working with This' or 'Working with That'). Rules can be created on task level, so that you'll be automatically reminded after a specific period of time.

Features as Comments, Reminders, Statistics and Plugins makes your life easier! This tour will cover the most basic functions in Hours and Minutes.
