Tasks & Comments

Tasks are userdefined items (ie. 'Working with This' may be one task, and 'Working with That' may be another). All tasks has their own timetable, allowing you to know exactly what you was doing one day.

Hours and Minutes will let you create your own tasks, and additionally you may even create Rules, bound to any of the tasks, to remind you after a specified period of time.

Here are some examples of tasks that you could create:
  • Painting in Photoshop
  • Surfing the Web
  • Playing Games

    Or, perhaps more serious:
  • Customer A
  • Customer B
  • Customer C

    Creating a Task
    1. Bring up the main window. Do this by double-clicking the Hours and Minutes icon in the tray-area of your startmenu. Or do this by rightclicking the tray-area icon, and selecting "Hours and Minutes"

    2. Make sure that the "Preferences" tab is visible, then click the "Tasks" button, next select "Modify Tasks" on the menu.

    3. Create a new task by clicking the "New" button.

    4. When finished, click the OK button in the Tasks dialog to return to the main dialog.
    Switching to a Task
    1. Rightclick at the Hours and Minutes tray icon.
    2. Select the switch task menu item, next select the task that you want to switch to.

    A few words about comments

    Comments are useful to help you remind what exactly it was that you were doing at a specified time. Say that you've setup a task called "Working with Project A" - you might want to add comments for when you are "Writing Documentation", "Having a meeting with the staff", "Is creating the UML scheme" etc.

    Add a Comment
    1. Rightclick at the Hours and Minutes tray icon.
    2. Select the "Add Comment" menu item, enter your comment."
