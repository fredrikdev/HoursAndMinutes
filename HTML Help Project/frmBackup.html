Securing your Data

Your timedata is saved in your personal section of the windows registry. This mean that Windows will handle all security for you.

However, sometimes it may be necessary to save your data on in another location - if you fear a system crash, or you need to reinstall your computer.

Backing up your Data
  1. Bring up the main window. Do this by double-clicking the Hours and Minutes icon in the tray-area of your startmenu. Or do this by rightclicking the tray-area icon, and selecting "Hours and Minutes".
  2. Make sure that the "Preferences" tab is visible, then click the "Backup" button, follow the instructions on the screen.
Restoring your Data from a Backup
  1. Exit Hours and Minutes. Do this by right-clicking the Hours and Minutes icon in the tray-area of your startmenu. Next, select Exit.
  2. Double-click the Backup Registry file to merge it into your registry.
  3. Start Hours and Minutes.
Backups are saved as regular Regedit export files - This mean more advantages for you, since you don't accually need Hours and Minutes installed to restore a backup.
